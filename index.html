Getting to know me

The Story So Far

where it began

I come from Statistics and Computer Science, not as separate tracks, but as one lens. I learned early that data without context is noise, and that making sense of it requires both mathematical rigour and the ability to explain your reasoning clearly.

the turning point

Then came AI, then robotics. My work now sits at the intersection of data systems, embedded AI, and making technical ideas genuinely accessible. To a 10-year-old and to a hiring manager, equally.

what I do

At JR BOTICS, I work as a Technical Trainer and Product Development Lead. I design and deliver AI and Robotics curriculum across multiple Bengaluru schools, and I also lead prototype and product builds: Arduino and ESP32 systems, computer vision pipelines, ML-integrated hardware. The teaching and the building happen in the same room, which keeps both honest.

the research side

I've applied statistical and econometric methods to real-world datasets, including a research stint at the NSSO, Government of India, working on PLFS labour data. My academic foundation in regression, modelling and big data systems runs parallel to the hands-on work.

right now

MSc Data Analytics at Christ University, Bangalore. Building deeper foundations in ML systems, data engineering and applied research, while shipping actual products at work.

Open to roles where I can build things, analyse things, and explain both.
